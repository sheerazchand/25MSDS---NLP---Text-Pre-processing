üîß Preprocessing Steps Applied

1. Lowercasing

Convert all text to lowercase for consistency

Before: "I'm Having ANXIETY about My JOB"
After: "i'm having anxiety about my job"
2. Contraction Expansion

Expand contractions for better analysis

Before: "don't, it's, I'm"
After: "do not, it is, I am"

Found in 47.2% of posts

3. URL/Email Removal

Remove web links and email addresses

Before: "Check https://example.com"
After: "Check [URL]"

Found in 1.8% of posts

4. Number Normalization

Replace numbers with placeholder token

Before: "3 years ago"
After: "NUM years ago"

Found in 46.7% of posts

5. Punctuation Removal

Remove excessive punctuation

Before: "Help!!!"
After: "Help."

Found in 18.8% of posts

6. Whitespace Normalization

Remove extra spaces and newlines

Before: "text with spaces"
After: "text with spaces"
7. Tokenization

Split text into individual words

Before: "I have anxiety"
After: ['I', 'have', 'anxiety']
8. Stopword Removal

Remove 152 common English words

Before: "I have the anxiety"
After: ['anxiety']

Token count reduced by 53.3%

9. Stemming

Reduce words to root form

Before: "running, worried, feeling"
After: "run, worri, feel"
